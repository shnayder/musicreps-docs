### Description (4000 chars, shared across both stores)

Music Reps trains instant recall of music fundamentals — closing the gap between
"give me a second, I know this" and "already moving on."

There are musical skills you can do with some hesitation: locating a note on
your fretboard, figuring out what key has 4 flats, or listing the notes in an
Em7 chord. Doing reps makes them automatic, so you can focus on playing.

HOW IT WORKS

- Fast drills, many reps. Each skill asks rapid-fire questions to make the
  knowledge you already have automatic.
- Speed is the goal. When your response time drops from eight seconds to under
  one, you've stopped counting and started knowing.
- Spaced repetition. The app focuses on what you haven't mastered yet and brings
  back material before you forget it.
- No instrument needed. Use commuting, waiting, or any other spare minutes to
  advance your playing.
- A few minutes at a time. Designed for short sessions — two to five minutes, a
  few times a day, over months and years.

SKILLS

Music Theory
- Note & Interval Semitones — Convert between notes, intervals, and semitone
  numbers instantly.
- Semitone Math — Add and subtract semitones from any note.
- Interval Math — Move up and down by any interval from any note.
- Scale Degrees — Know every scale degree in every key.
- Diatonic Chords — Know every chord in every key.
- Chord Spelling — Instantly spell the notes in any chord.

Reading Music
- Key Signatures — Instantly know the sharps and flats in any key.

Guitar
- Fretboard — Name any fret on the guitar instantly.
- Speed Tap — Find every position of any note on the neck.
- Chord Shapes — Tap chord fingerings from memory.

Ukulele
- Fretboard — Name any fret on the ukulele instantly.
- Chord Shapes — Tap chord fingerings from memory.

FEATURES

- 13 skills across music theory, guitar, and ukulele
- Adaptive question selection with spaced repetition
- Per-item speed tracking so you can see exactly what you know
- Progress tracking with heatmap visualizations
- Recommendations that guide you from learning to mastery
- Keyboard support for desktop use
- Works offline — no account or internet required
- No ads. No subscriptions.
