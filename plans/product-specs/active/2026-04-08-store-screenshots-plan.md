# Store Screenshot Plan

## Story Arc

The screenshots should walk someone through a narrative:

1. **Hook** — what is this? (home screen showing breadth of modes)
2. **Show** — what does training look like? (active quiz)
3. **Prove** — does it work? (progress tracking, mastery)
4. **Expand** — what else can I train? (variety of modes)

The overall message: **reps build automatic fundamentals so you can focus on playing.**

## Key Brand Points to Reinforce

From the brand guide — each screenshot should hit at least one:

- **Automatic recall** — "Train until it's automatic", "instant recall"
- **Fast drills** — "Fast drills designed for many reps"
- **Practice anywhere** — "Turn spare minutes into real practice"
- **Breadth** — 11 training modes, not just one thing
- **Progress** — you can see yourself improving

Messaging tone: focused, serious, minimal. Not playful/gamified.

## Tooling

Marketing assets are generated by `deno task marketing`. Config lives in
`scripts/marketing-manifest.ts`. Templates in `scripts/marketing-templates/`.

To iterate on screenshot selection:
1. Edit `MARKETING_ASSETS` array in `marketing-manifest.ts`
2. Run `deno task marketing` (or `--skip-raw` to reuse cached screenshots)
3. Open `screenshots/marketing/index.html` to review
